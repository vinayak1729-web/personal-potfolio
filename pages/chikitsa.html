Overview

C.H.I.K.I.T.S.A is an AI-powered mental health chatbot designed to assist individuals in their mental health journey. It leverages cutting-edge technologies like natural language processing, medical image analysis, and voice interaction to provide personalized mental health assessments and emotional support.
